Rules Overview

Goal

Reach 15 Fame to win. Creatures generate fame when unblocked in battle.


Each Round

  1. Draft phase: Each player swaps 1 card with the market (×3 times)
  2. Play phase: Each player may play 1 card after each draft
  3. Battle: All creatures act simultaneously
  4. Cleanup: Discard hand, draw 5, refresh 3 energy

Energy

You have 3 energy per round. Spend it to play creature and spell cards.


Lanes (1–4)

Place creatures in lanes. Creatures in the same lane fight each other. Unblocked creatures generate fame instead of dealing damage.


Upgrade

Discard a card matching a creature you control (same name) to place a +1 counter on it. Free action, no energy cost.


Counters

+1 counter: +1 ATK, +1 Fame, +1 HP
-1 counter: -1 ATK, -1 Fame, -1 HP
Shield: Absorbs one damage instance
Damage markers persist between rounds
